Los Angeles Golfers face a unique challenge with limited time and scarce access to quality courses. with few public golf options and high demand for tee times, booking a round can be frustrating and time consuming.

Par 4 offers a convenient, high-tech alternative, allowing golfers to practice, play, and improve their game on-demand without the hassle of securing a tee time or committing to hours on the course.

The indoor golf simulator market is experiencing rapid growth, valued at nearly $1.5 billion in 2022 and projected to nearly double by 2030. with a rising demand for year-round golfing experiences, particularly among a new generation of players, establishing a golf simulator facility presents a prime opportunity to tap into this expanding market and build a loyal customer base for long-term success.

Par 4 is launching a seed round to open its first premier indoor golf facility in Los Angeles, addressing the city’s growing demand for accessible golf experiences. Par 4 offers a modern, on-demand alternative, combining state-of-the-art simulators, performance tracking, and a social lounge environment. This funding round will support construction, technology, and brand expansion, laying the foundation for future growth in the West Coast’s emerging indoor golf market.
